Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and how you can control them on this website.

What Cookies Are

  • Cookies are small files stored in your browser.
  • They help the website remember basic settings and preferences.
  • Some cookies are essential for core site features.

How We Use Them

  • Keep the site working smoothly and securely.
  • Understand general site usage and performance.
  • Improve content, navigation and user experience.

Your Choices

  • Allow all cookies if you want the full experience.
  • Reject all non-essential cookies if you prefer.
  • Read this page anytime from the footer link.

Managing Cookies

Most browsers let you block or delete cookies from the settings menu. If you change your mind later, you can revisit the consent banner or clear your browser data to reset preferences.

  • Choose how your browser handles cookies.
  • Delete stored cookies from your device at any time.
  • Contact the branch if you need help understanding the policy.

Policy Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy when our website changes or when legal requirements change. The latest version on this page always replaces earlier versions.

  • Review the page from time to time.
  • New versions will be reflected here first.
  • Contact us with any privacy-related question.
